How do we make Cinnamon Rolls?

A delciosuly glazed Cinnamon Roll

The recipe is a delcious combination of simple ingredients that can be easily prepared by virtually anyone! It requires a minimal amount of effort and time to make, but it's incredibly important to pay strict attention to the recipe for the best taste.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Molasses
  1. Make the dough, stirr the ingredients together.
  2. Make the cinnamon filling, stirring the Molasses, Cinnamon, And sugar together.
  3. Make the glaze, using cream, milk, and lots of sugar.
  4. Put them together and start baking!
