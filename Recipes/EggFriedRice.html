How do we make Egg Fried Rice?

A Egg Fried Rice

Egg fried is an incredibly simple dish, that so many people still manage to mess up. Make sure you put the ingredients in at the right time!

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Rice
  • Spring onion
  • Soy sauce
  1. Cook the rice.
  2. Add the eggs in.
  3. Add soy sauce and more seasoning.
  4. Add the spring onions!
