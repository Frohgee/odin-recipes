How do we make sandwiches?

The best sandwich ever

Sandwiches are easier to make, quick to prepare, and tasty. This recipe ensures all three criteria are met!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Sauce
  1. Toast the bread(or not).
  2. Add the meat onto one slice of bread
  3. Add some vegetables on top of the meat
  4. Finish it off with some sauce and put the other slice on top!
